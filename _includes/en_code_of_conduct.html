Code of Conduct

All events are covered under the Berlin Code of Conduct. Additionally, steps will be taken to ensure the privacy and safety of members attending. If you see a CoC violation or have questions, you can contact any of the organisers in person or via email contact@codecurious.org or via Twitter @codecurious_bln. If you would prefer to speak directly with individual organizers, you can contact these people: Ferdous (English/Deutsch) by email itrytocode@gmail.com and Twitter @ferbsx, or Yonatan (English) by email yonatan@shuhugah.com and Twitter @shushugah or Kaja (Deutsch/English) by email kaja@codecurious.org and Twitter @AlizeNero.
